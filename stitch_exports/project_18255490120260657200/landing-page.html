The Methodology

Bridging the gap between spoken word and archived human knowledge.

At its core, "Which Quote?" isn't just an assistant; it's a digital curator. By mapping the vast landscape of Wikiquote into a multi-dimensional graph database, we enable semantic jumps that traditional databases cannot perform.

Whether you're writing a thesis or debating in a classroom, our real-time speaker recognition ensures that the context is always respected, and the attributions are always accurate.
